Detailed Description

A with directive (e.g. <div data-with="sales"><span data-text="sum(quantity*price)" /></div> )

The expression in the data-with attribute is evaluated and made the subject of a new context namespace. All child nodes are rendered within the new namespace. The namespace is then exited.
